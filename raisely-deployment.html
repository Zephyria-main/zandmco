MEET THE FOUNDER: ZARA

Hi, I'm Zara – Founder of Z and M Co.

In 2023, when I was just 8 years old, I started Z and M Co. to support women in brain cancer research. After my first stall, Australian National University (ANU) created the Zara Fund for Women in Brain Cancer Research in response to my initiative. The Zara Fund helps cover childcare and supports female researchers to travel to conferences, so they can keep doing their life-saving brain cancer research, while raising their families, especially mums trying to balance science and family.
